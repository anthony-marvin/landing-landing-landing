The Procedure

One of the most popular ways to correct vision is with a procedure called LASIK (laser in-situ keratomileusis) or laser eye surgery, which uses a laser to change the curvature of the cornea (outer window of the eye). 

LASIK removes tissue within the cornea to treat low to high levels of nearsightedness, farsightedness and astigmatism. 

Stahl Eyecare Experts now uses the Intralase iFS laser in conjuction with the Star s4 excimer laser utilizing both the CustomVue and iDesign wavefront mapping technology to perform laser eye surgery. 

LASIK is a very safe, quick and easy alternative to wearing glasses or contacts. However, there are a lot of LASIK myths out there, and at times it can be difficult to separate fact from fiction.
