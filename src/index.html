What is LASIK?

Imagine waking up and seeing the numbers on your alarm clock clearly, or reading the fine print on a menu without fumbling for glasses. Since 1991, over 21 million people have found this freedom thanks to laser vision correction procedures like LASIK.

There are currently seven laser-based procedures, including LASIK, that can be used to correct a range of vision problems. You owe it to yourself to find out which one is best for you. Brinton Vision is one of only a handful of LASIK surgery centers in the U.S. to offer LASIK plus all six LASIK alternatives under one roof.

Why Brinton Vision?

Dr. Jason P. Brinton is a Harvard graduate and fellowship-trained physician who has helped thousands of patients achieve visual freedom without the hassle of glasses or contacts. He is considered by his peers to be a pioneer in laser vision correction and routinely trains surgeons on modern refractive procedures. He continues to invest in education, state-of-the-art technology, and staff training to ensure his patients receive the customized, personalized care they deserve.

For more than two decades, these physicians have performed over 40,000 laser vision correction procedures. They are experts in caring for vision and for their patients, believing that we live better and safer lives when we see better. As a community service initiative, they performed over 500 LASIK procedures on New York’s firefighters. Helping them see better, without being dependent on glasses or contacts, helps all of us to be safer.
